Our Pre-Wedding Story

On April 3rd, 2025, we returned to Paris from Brussels to capture a very special moment — our pre-wedding photos.

It marked 10 years of love, both of us turning 30, and having our baby. Paris has always been a place full of memories — from our student days to now as a family.

This city witnessed many milestones in our lives. We both studied here — my husband started his Master’s at 23, and I joined him later for my own studies while he pursued his PhD. Despite the pandemic in 2020, we shared unforgettable years together in this beautiful city.

Paris is also where we officially registered our marriage at the Vietnamese Embassy. Returning this time, not just as a couple but as parents with our 7-month-old son, felt like closing a beautiful full circle.

This photo session is more than a keepsake — it’s our way of celebrating a journey of love, growth, and meaningful milestones.
